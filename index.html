Brown University Washing Machine
Research and Observations

Why examine the washing machine?

  • It's a public interface shared by all students of a dorm
  • It contains multiple moving parts that require different types of interactions (button, handles, trays, etc.)
  • It's used differently by each person, depending on their preferences (detergent, pod, frequency)
  • A single machine can be used by multiple people at once during turnovers
